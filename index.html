Madarjoon's recipies

Welcome to my Grandma's Recipie where I ask my grandma to teach me her timeless delicious Persian recipies.

I don't really know how to cook but NOBODY in the family makes these dished better than Madarjoon and I just have to learn them.

I want to be able to make these dishes after she's gone as a way to honour and remember her.

Although these dishes probably won't have the same sentimental value to you, I assure you that if you put the time to make them, they will absolutely be worth it.

Disclaimer: These are Persian recipies. They might taste weird to you.
